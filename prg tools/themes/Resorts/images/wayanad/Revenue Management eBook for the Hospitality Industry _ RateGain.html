  • Are you an avid nature lover and an explorer? If yes, then in the northeastern part of god's own country Kerala lies the splendid Wayanad, which is an idyllic travelling destination for the vacationers as well as the business travelers.

    Wayanad is a sightseers delight with its quaint hills and its close rendezvous with nature. The wild  forests of Western Ghats along with the mesmerizing waterfalls,  rivers, jungle trails, reservoirs, tree houses, the  out of the ordinary wildlife and historical monuments create an irresistible  land which is famously called Wayanad.

    This charming place of Wayanad is a major centre of attraction for numerous tourists across the globe every year and that is why resorts in Wayanad are beautiful,, which fit into the budget and are pocket friendly for everyone.

    Some of the well-known and very famous attractions in the vicinity of Wayanad are  Meenmutty Falls,  Neelimala viewpoint,  Pakshipathalam,  Banasura Sagar Dam, Chembra Peak trek, Chethalayam Falls, Kuruva Island,  Pookote Lake, Pazhassi Park, Edakkal Caves and Wayanad Heritage Museum.
